WebiRTC for Chrome


Welcome to WebRTC communication proof of concept for TOPIC...

Give a try !

Requirement

This code is currently designed to work with Chrome browser only.
Mozilla Firefox and Opera compatibility should be ready soon.
What about Internet Explorer and Safari, WebRTC functionalities are simply not currently supported.

In case you do not get any video from your webcam, it is likely to be due to a resolution incompatibility between camera and web browser.

How to use

Open two pages with this same url http://webrtc.webinage.net/webrtc (each on a different machine, or even both on the same one).
One of those will play the part of the caller, and the other one the part of the callee.
On each page, click on the green "Give a try !" button or on "WebRTC" on the top-right of the page to access the page designed for WebRTC.
Chose the same room name for both caller and callee page (room space is limited to a unique caller-callee couple). On one of the two pages you have chosen as the caller page, click on the "Start" green button and grant your web browser access to your webcam.
Then click on "Call" button on its right to initiate the call.
A popup should now appear on callee page, which indicates you are being called by a peer and gives you instructions to allow the communication.
Follow the instruction by allowing the callee browser on callee page to access the callee webcam.
You are done, you should now see on both peer two videos, on on the left of yourself and one on the right of your peer !
It is also possible to send text messages with the text boxes on the bottom of your local video to your peer.
To conclude, you can also put an end to the call by clicking on "Hung Up" button on caller or callee page.
